FAQs

Do I have to have a reservation or can I walk in and get a rental?

We do accept walk-ins but rentals may be limited based on the number of reservations for the day. Walk-in rentals also cost a little bit more than reservations.

What's the easiest way to make a reservation if I am coming on a cruise?

You can either book through your cruiseline or here on our website. Both ways are easy and convenient

Can you bring my rentals to me at my hotel?

We do deliver rentals for reservations that are 2+ days long. We will bring your rental and drop it off directly at your hotel, airBnB, or wherever you are staying. We typically set an hour window and dropoff your rental within that hour.

Can I pickup my rental at one location and dropoff at another?

Yes! It's completely fine to pickup at one of our locations and to dropoff at the other. We want to make the rental process as convenient for you as possible.

Is there any age restrictions with your rentals?

Yes, there are age restrictions. You must be at least 18 years old to rental one of our scooters, jeeps, or ATVs. For liabilty reasons, nobody under the age of 12 is allowed to ride on the scooters.

How late can I make a reservation?

Reservations require at least 24 hours notice. After 24 hours, walk-in fees will be assessed.

What do I need in order to complete my rental?

We require that you provide a valid driver's license at the time of rental. Payment is also due at the time of the rental. We accept Google pay, cash, credit card, debit card, or PayPay.
