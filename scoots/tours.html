Welcome to Cozumel!

Cozumel is an island off of the east coast of Mexico’s Yucatan Peninsula. The island is a major tourist destination and is visited by people from around the world. Most people arrive by cruise or by ferry from nearby Playa Del Carmen or Cancun Mexico. Cozumel offers a variety of adventures and is known as one of the best snorkeling or scuba diving destinations in the world. The island is rich with history having been occupied by the Mayan and later the Spanish Explorers. At Scoots, we want to help you explore everything the island has to offer!

Things to Do in Cozumel

Scuba Diving and Snorkling

Cozumel is home to some of the best snorkeling and scuba diving in the world. The majority of the island is surrounded by coral reef which is home to over 1,000 species of fish. The western and southern coasts offer a variety of diving locations. Whether you’re an experienced diver or if you’re new to the activity, Cozumel has you covered. A variety of scuba and snorkeling tours are available for people of all skill levels.

Mayan Ruins

Cozumel is home to several Mayan ruins. Though not as large as the ruins across the Yucatan Channel in Cancun and Playa Del Carmenl, the ruins in Cozumel are still a site to behold. You can visit San Gervasio on the north end of the island and Zona Arqueológica El Caracol on the south end. Both sites offer guided tours where you can learn more about Mayan history and culture.

Jeep and ATV Trails
