Welcome to Cozumel!

Welcome to Cozumel Mexico! Here at Scoots, we’re excited to help you explore island whether you’re visiting for a few days, stopping by on a cruise, or if you’ve lived here for years. Cozumel offers a wide variety of activities across the island and we want to help you get there. Whether you’re looking for a romantic drive for two on one of our double-seated scooters or an off-roading adventure in one of our Jeep Wranglers, Scoots has something to offer you. Many tourists never see outside of the west side of the island, but with one of our rentals, you can get off the beaten path and see a side of Cozumel that others rarely get to see. If you're looking to stay for several hours or several days, we can accomodate you. We have two locations where you can pickup and return your rentals, both conveniently located near main cruise and ferry terminals. Staying for more than two days? Let us know and we can bring your rental directly to your hotel, airBnB, or wherever you’re staying. Place your reservation today and get exploring!
