Target Audience

Target Audience Description

www.weatherornot.io will target outdoor enthusiasts and adventure seekers. It's target audience is anyone who loves the adventure of the great outdoors. This includes people who enjoy skiing, hiking, camping, river rafting, rock climbing, fishing, hunting, or anything of that nature. When planning an outdoor adventure, these individuals need to be prepared for whatever the weather is going to throw at them.

Personas

Persona One: We will target young adults between the age of 18-40 who are avid outdoorsmen. Since the site is focused primarily in southeast Idaho, we will focus on these young adults in the surrounding states including Idaho, Utah, Wyoming, Montana and Colorado. They will have median incomes over anywhere between $30K and $75K a year. We also focus on both single and married people. These individuals will be hardcore outdoorsman. They live for getting out into the wild.

Persona Two: Our second persona will be a little more lax than the first. This persona will focus on middle aged individuals with children. They will be in age between 30 - 55 and will have a family. This persona is people who love getting outside and being with their family. They're not as hardcore as they once were, but they love teaching their children about being in the outdoors.

Scenarios

  1. A college student is wondering wether or not to plan a ski trip this weekend. The last ski trip was incredible icy and not fun. Is it going to snow this week and should they plan the trip?
  2. A small family of four is wanting to go camping this weekend, but they're not sure what the weather will be? What's the liklihood that rain will ruin their camping trip?
  3. Is it a good time to raft the snake river? You want to raft, but also don't want to be burnt to a crisp? What day this week would have the most cloud coverage for your adventure?
  4. You want to teach your son how to golf, but it's spring and the weather has been unpredictable lately. What's the best day this week to go out?
  5. You're going out to ride dirt bikes this weekend. The area is really dusty so it'd be nice to go a day or two after it rains. What's the best day to go this week?
