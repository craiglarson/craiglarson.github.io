Site Info

Site Name:

Weather or Not

URL

www.weatherornot.io

Site Purpose:

The purpose of WeatherOrNot.io is to help outdoorsman and adventure seekers to know "weather or not" the weather will present perfect conditions for their outdoors activities. Wether that's looking for the perfect snowday for a trip to the slopes or looking for sunshine to float the nearest river, WeatherOrNot.io will help adventurers to know if their plans are a go. The site will provide users with the latest weather forecasts and also give recommendations for activities that will be perfect for the conditions in the forecast, that way they'll have the best adventure possible.
