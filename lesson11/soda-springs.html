Camping Near Soda Springs

by Craig Larson

Camping is a great summer activity and provides an opportunity to create memories with family and friends. Whether you’re looking for an established campground or if you’re looking to get off the beaten path, there are plenty of camping spots near Soda Springs.

There are plenty of campgrounds within a short driving distance of Soda Springs and each offers a variety of amenities. Summit View, Diamond Creek, and Mill Canyon campgrounds are all east of town and are accessible with a quick hour drive or less. To the south of town, you can visit the Cold Springs and Eightmile campgrounds.

In addition to established campgrounds, there are also plenty of primitive spots to camp. There are plenty of trails and backpacking opportunities in the mountains surrounding Soda Springs.

If you’re looking for something a little more comfortable than roughing it on the ground, Lava Hot Springs is a quick 23-minute drive away. There are additional campsites near Lava. You can spend the day floating the river or taking a dip in the hot springs at the center of town.

The weather in Soda Springs will be perfect for camping this week! Temperatures will be in the high 70s with clouds at the beginning of the week, but clearing up near the end of the week. Grab your friends, your tent, and get outside this weekend!
