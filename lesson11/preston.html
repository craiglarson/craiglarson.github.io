Summer Fishing Near Preston Idaho

by Craig Larson

Summer is the perfect time to get out, grab your fishing pole, and hit the nearby lakes or rivers with your friends and family. Luckily, Preston provides many great opportunities for the avid or casual fishermen or fisherwomen. If you’re looking to catch that trophy bass or just to create memories with your kids, Preston has the perfect fishing experiences for you!

So where are the best places to go fishing near Preston? Really, it’s your choice, because there are plenty of fishing waters nearby. Foster, Lamont, Johnson, and Glendale Reservoirs are all a quick 10-minute drive from town. If you want to venture out a bit further, you can head to Twin Lakes, Condie, and Treasure Reservoirs. If you enjoy fly-fishing on a river, Bear River is only a few minutes away as well.

Each of these bodies of water provides great fishing opportunities whether you’re an avid fisherman or just getting started. You’ll have the chance to catch a variety of fish including trout, largemouth bass, smallmouth bass, bluegill, perch, and walleye.

The next few weeks will provide perfect fishing weather in the Preston area. Skies will be sunny and the temperatures will peak in the low 80s, so grab your gear and head to the nearest lake soon!
