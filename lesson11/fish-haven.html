Vacationing at Bear Lake

by Craig Larson

Looking to get out of the city and create memories with your family this summer? Bear Lake is the perfect destination for you! Bear Lake is an 18.33-mile-long lake near Fish Haven that provides a variety of different water activities. Weather it’s tubing behind a boat, taking a leisurely row on a paddleboard, or hunting for that trophy fish that you’ve never caught, Bear Lake has what you’re looking for!

Famous for its turquoise-blue water, Bear Lake provides opportunities for many different water activities. You can take a boat out on the lake for some waterskiing, wakeboarding, wake surfing r tubing. Don’t have a boat? There are plenty of rental places where you can get a boat or jet ski for the day. If you’re looking for a more relaxing activity, there are beach areas along the lake where you can lounge, swim, and take the kids for a kick kayak or paddle board ride.

If you’re hungry after a day on the lake, hop over to La Beau’s for a famous raspberry shake. If you’re not in the mood for a shake, there are plenty of other places to eat including Firehouse Pizzeria, Cooper’s Restaurant, and the Campfire Grill.

The weather will be nice and sunny for the next few days with highs in the mid-70s. The best time to get out on the lake will be in the middle of the day, when the sun is at its peak. Get out there and have some fun!
